About Restaurant
MUSWELL HILL
Tucked away in the charming neighbourhood of Muswell Hill,
Akatuki opened its doors in late 2023, quickly earning the affection of local residents.

Combining traditional Japanese flavours with a modern twist, it offers a unique dining experience that keeps guests returning time and again. Each dish is thoughtfully crafted, presenting a perfect balance of authenticity and creativity. From a carefully curated selection of artisanal sake to innovative cocktails inspired by the essence of Japan, Akatuki promises a truly memorable culinary journey.

This first location established the core of Akatuki’s ethos: more than just dining, it’s about celebrating culture, flavour, and the excitement of new beginnings. It set the stage for what Akatuki aspires to be—bringing the heart of Japan to London with a touch of local warmth.

COVENT GARDEN
Following the success of its Muswell Hill location, Akatuki brought its signature charm to the vibrant streets of Covent Garden in late 2024.

  • With a focus on premium, meticulously sourced ingredients and artistic presentation, this second location redefines the Japanese dining experience with a sense of elegance and sophistication. The interiors reflect a harmonious blend of traditional Japanese aesthetics and contemporary design, creating a serene yet stylish atmosphere that invites diners to savour every moment.

    From intricately handcrafted sushi that captures the essence of the sea to indulgent bowls of sukiyaki bursting with flavour, every dish at Akatuki Covent Garden is a work of art. This location not only elevates Japanese cuisine but also solidifies Akatuki’s reputation as a must-visit destination for food enthusiasts in London.
