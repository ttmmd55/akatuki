About Restaurant
COVENT GARDEN
After winning the hearts of Muswell Hill, Akatuki arrived in Covent Garden in late 2024—bringing its signature charm to the heart of London.

Akatuki started in Muswell Hill—a cosy neighbourhood spot where locals gathered for steaming bowls of sukiyaki and fresh, hand-rolled sushi. After a year of warm reviews and full tables, we brought that same passion to Covent Garden in late 2024, refining our craft for the heart of London.

Here, every detail is elevated. Premium ingredients, elegant presentation, and a space that blends Japanese tradition with modern city life. From silky sashimi to rich, simmering hot pots, every dish is designed to be savoured.

Akatuki Covent Garden isn’t just a meal—it’s an experience for those who love Japanese food done right.

MUSWELL HILL
Akatuki first opened in Muswell Hill in late 2023, quickly becoming a neighbourhood favourite.

  • Akatuki began in late 2023 as a neighbourhood favourite, tucked away in Muswell Hill. It wasn’t about being flashy—just good food, warm service, and a space where locals could unwind over comforting bowls of sukiyaki and sizzling hot stone grills.

    The mix of tradition and creativity quickly made it a go-to spot. Carefully crafted sushi, slow-braised broths, a selection of artisanal sake—it all came together to create something special.

    More than just a restaurant, Akatuki Muswell Hill is where good food meets good company. It’s where our story began.
